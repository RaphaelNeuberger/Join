Overview of Processing

This Privacy Policy explains which personal data we collect, why we collect it and how we use and protect it when you use the Join product and related services. We only process personal data necessary for the specified purposes and in accordance with applicable law.

  • Security, fraud prevention, and protection of our systems.
  • Improvement of the product through analytics and usage statistics.
  • Optional marketing or newsletter communication, where you have given consent.

Types of Data We Collect

Depending on how you use Join, we may collect:

  • Account data: name, email address, username (if you register).

  • Usage data: pages visited, actions taken within the app, and anonymized analytics.
  • Communication data: messages you send to our support or contact forms.

Legal Bases for Processing

We rely on the following legal bases under the GDPR when applicable:

  • Performance of a contract: to provide the services you requested.
  • Consent: where you have given consent (e.g., newsletters, certain cookies).
  • Legitimate interests: for fraud prevention, security, and service improvement, balanced against your rights.
  • Legal obligation: where the law requires us to retain or disclose information.

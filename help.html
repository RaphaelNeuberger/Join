Welcome to the Join Help Page! Here you will find everything you need to know about using the Join Kanban Project Management Tool.

What is Join?

Join is a digital Kanban-style project management application that helps you and your team organize tasks, manage deadlines, and collaborate efficiently. You can create, edit, and assign tasks across your projects.

Main Features

  • Summary: See an overview of your current tasks, progress, and deadlines.
  • Board: Manage your tasks visually using the Kanban board. Move tasks between columns like “To Do”, “In Progress”, and “Done”.
  • Add Task: Create new tasks with title, description, due date, priority, and assigned contacts.
  • Contacts: Manage your team members, view their details, and assign them to tasks easily.

How to Use Join

  1. Click on Add Task to create a new task.
  2. Assign your task to one or multiple contacts.
  3. Set a priority and a due date.
  4. Use the Board to track progress by dragging tasks between stages.
  5. Review your status and statistics on the Summary page.

Tips for Productivity

  • Update your board daily to keep the overview clear.
  • Communicate with your team regularly via your chosen channels.
  • Keep your tasks small and focused to make progress measurable.
  • Use color codes and priorities to visualize urgency.
